Welcome to Marvel Smash!

Marvel Smash is a fast-paced card game. Choose your hero, manage your Action Points (AP), and place cards on the board to defeat your opponent!

How to Play:

  • Heroes: Each player chooses a Marvel hero with a unique passive ability and specialized card deck.
  • Action Points (AP): You gain AP every round (up to a max cap). Use AP to play cards from your hand.
  • Cards: Play cards to the staging area. Trade and Hybrid cards deal damage instantly during the Combat Phase. Summons will spawn onto your board to defend you!
  • Combat: When both players end their turn, the Combat Phase begins. Cards attack automatically. Summons act as a shield to protect your Hero's HP.
  • Victory: Deplete your opponent's Hero HP to 0 to win the match!
